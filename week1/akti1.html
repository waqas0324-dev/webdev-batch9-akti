Quaid-e- Azam Biography

Quaid-e-Azam

Birth: 25 Dec 1876

Place: karachi

Father : Jinnah

Died : 11 Dec 1948 at karachi

History

Born at Wazir Mansion in Karachi, Jinnah was trained as a barrister at Lincoln's Inn in London, England. Upon his return to India, he enrolled at the Bombay High Court,Bombay High Court, and took an interest in national politics, which eventually replaced his legal practice.
